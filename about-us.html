About Us

It all started with a vision: To bring stylish and digital products with an essence of India, to the world.

 

We aimed at all who like to make a style statement everywhere they go.
We offer world class functional features, the brand is committed to help you Move in Style through vibrant and unique designs that add an edge to the world personality. Behind the stylish aesthetics, lies over four decades of design and engineering innovation, resulting in products that are as durable and reliable as they are stylish!

Apart from being the first Indian brand to manufacture all Digital products, We also manufactures a wide variety of products like smart phone, all digital products, tablets, laptop, travel accessories and short haul essentials made from quality materials.

IKKO hails from VIP Industries (est. 1971), market leaders for over 50 years and shares its roots with sister brands like VIP, Carlton and Caprese.
